# Afterglow: Guarding the Last Warmth in the Digital Wasteland

## Our Origin: Solving the Paradox of Forgetting

In the 21st century, we live dual lives: one physical, defined by breath and heartbeat; the other digital, defined by code, pixels, and encryption keys. We store memories on social media, essential documents in the cloud, and wealth in digital wallets.

However, a harsh reality is often ignored: when life ends in the physical world, our digital presence often falls into a permanent silence. Locked by master passwords and inaccessible private keys, these assets become "Digital Wastelands."

## The Birth of Afterglow: Bridging Time and Space

We chose the name Afterglow because we believe a person's value should not cease the moment their breathing stops. Like the gentle glow that lingers after the sun sets, your legacy should continue to illuminate and guide those you love. Afterglow is not just about "data transfer"; it is about honoring a final trust.
